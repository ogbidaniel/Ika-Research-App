Advancing Speech Recognition
For The Ika Language

We build a speech recognition model for the Ika language. Using just one hour of labeled audio data, we demonstrate that finetuning multilingual pretrained models is very effective for developing speech recognition tools for Low resource languages. We tune Meta AI's Open-Weight Massively Multilingual Speech (MMS) models. This research aims to address the scarcity of labelled data for the Ika language and enhance digital inclusivity and language preservation
